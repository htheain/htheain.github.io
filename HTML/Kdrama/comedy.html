Mr. Queen follows a modern-day chef whose soul is mysteriously transported into the body of Queen Cheorin during the Joseon era, forcing him to navigate palace politics, concealed royal agendas, and a scheming power bloc while searching for a way back to his own life. Known for blending sharp historical intrigue with over-the-top body-swap comedy, the series showcases vibrant period costumes, witty dialogue, and standout performances from Shin Hye-sun and Kim Jung-hyun. Praised for its humor, creativity, and high production values, it achieved record ratings and strong critical acclaim.
