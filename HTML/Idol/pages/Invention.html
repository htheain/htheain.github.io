Inventions

Flying Machine

Leonardo da Vinci designed a flying machine inspired by birds. This invention showed his understanding of flight and aerodynamics.


Armored Vehicle

Leonardo designed an armored vehicle to protect soldiers in battle. It is considered an early idea of the modern tank.


Parachute

Leonardo sketched a parachute centuries before modern parachutes were invented. His design focused on safe descent from the air.


Mechanical Knight

Leonardo created plans for a mechanical knight that could move its arms, legs, and head. It is considered one of the earliest robot concepts.
